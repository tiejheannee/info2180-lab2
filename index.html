Why Cybersecurity

September 19, 2025 by Tiejheannee Gibson

Cybersecurity is one of the most important fields in today’s digital world because almost every part of our lives is connected to technology. From banking and healthcare to communication and entertainment, sensitive data is constantly being stored and shared online. Without proper protection, that information could easily fall into the wrong hands. I see cybersecurity as a way to defend people, businesses, and even entire nations from threats that can cause financial loss, identity theft, and major disruptions.

I’m drawn to cybersecurity not just because it’s challenging, but because it makes a real difference. It’s about problem-solving, staying one step ahead of hackers, and ensuring that technology can be trusted. With cybercrime growing every year, I believe learning and working in this field means contributing to a safer, more secure digital future. That sense of purpose is what motivates me to pursue cybersecurity and keep developing my skills.
